## Patents Practiced by This Work

The following patent applications, authored by Santiago Maniches
(ORCID: [0009-0005-6480-1987](https://orcid.org/0009-0005-6480-1987))
and assigned to TOPOLOGICA LLC, are practiced by this Work:

1. **Drift tensor correction framework** for high-precision structural
   property regression (validated R² = 0.9992).
2. **Topological fingerprinting of protein structures** via persistent

## Reservation of Rights

Apache 2.0 §3 grants patent rights only insofar as they are practiced
"by combination of their Contribution(s) with the Work." Patent rights
to practice the methods above **outside** of this Work — for instance,
by re-implementing them in a competing product that does not
incorporate this Work — are **reserved** and require a separate
license.

If you are uncertain whether your use is covered, write to
`patents@topologica.ai` and ask. We answer in good faith.

## Defensive Termination

Apache 2.0 §3 also provides that if a user files patent litigation
alleging that the Work infringes a patent, the user's patent rights
under Apache 2.0 terminate. This Project will assert that clause when
necessary to protect its contributors and users.
