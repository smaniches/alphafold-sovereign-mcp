## Patent applications practiced by this work

The following patent applications, authored by Santiago Maniches
(ORCID: [0009-0005-6480-1987](https://orcid.org/0009-0005-6480-1987)),
are practiced by this Work:

1. **Drift-tensor correction framework** for structural-property
   regression.
2. **Topological fingerprinting of protein structures** via
   persistent homology over Vietoris-Rips filtrations of Cα backbone
   coordinates, with Wasserstein- and bottleneck-comparable
   signatures.
3. **Hybrid local-first retrieval architecture** for AlphaFold
   structures combining a dynamically indexed local cache with a
   public-database fallback and content-addressed verification.

this Work and Derivative Works thereof.

Apache 2.0 §3 grants patent rights only insofar as they are practiced
"by combination of their Contribution(s) with the Work." Patent
rights to practice the methods above **outside** of this Work — for
instance, by re-implementing them in a competing product that does
not incorporate this Work — are **reserved** by the author.

If you are uncertain whether your use is covered, open a
[GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/smaniches/alphafold-sovereign-mcp/discussions)
and ask.

Apache 2.0 §3 also provides that if a user files patent litigation
alleging that the Work infringes a patent, the user's patent rights
under Apache 2.0 terminate.
